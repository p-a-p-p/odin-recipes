Kinilaw

Kinilaw - a FIlipino dish of raw tuna

One of my favorite food. Never absent in any occasion. A delicious dish made up of raw tuna. Sometimes used for inom.

Ingredients

  • Raw Tuna
  • Chili
  • Garlic
  • MSG
  • Magic Sarap
  • Cucumber
  • Ginger
  • Calamansi
  • Vinegar
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Prepare your raw tuna in a bowl. Then put it in the freezer.
  2. Cut ginger
  3. prepare cucumber, garlic, onion
  4. Without the tuna, mix all of the ingredients in a bowl of your choice
  5. Add the cold raw tuna in the bowl of mixture
  6. eat
